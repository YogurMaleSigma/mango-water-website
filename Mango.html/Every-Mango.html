The Wonderful World of Mangos

Types of Mangos

Alphonso Mango

Known for its sweetness and richness, often called the king of mangoes.

Haden Mango

A popular variety with a rich flavor and bright color.

Kent Mango

Sweet and juicy, perfect for eating fresh or in smoothies.

Tommy Atkins Mango

Widely available and known for its long shelf life.

Osean Mango

A hybrid variety known for its sweetness and aroma.

Pairi Mango

A sweet mango with a unique flavor, often enjoyed in summer.

Mangifera Mango

A lesser-known variety with a unique taste and texture.

Himalaya Mango

Sweet and fragrant, it grows in the foothills of the Himalayas.

Apple Mango

Crisp and sweet, often used in salads and desserts.

Bombay Mango

Known for its vibrant taste and rich texture.

Palma Mango

A sweet variety popular in tropical regions.

Bourbon Mango

Known for its rich flavor and aromatic sweetness, popular in desserts.

Chaunsa Mango

Sweet and fragrant, it's a favorite during the summer in South Asia.

Kesar Mango

Known for its sweetness and bright orange flesh, often used in desserts.

Sweet Heart Mango

A small, juicy variety known for its rich flavor and sweetness.

Lassi Mango

Often used in the popular mango lassi drink, creamy and refreshing.

Rose Mango

Famous for its floral aroma and sweet taste, often enjoyed fresh.

Honey Mango

Known for its sweet, honey-like flavor, popular in tropical regions.

Dushehari Mango

A sweet variety known for its juicy flesh and rich flavor.

Langra Mango

A tangy and sweet variety, popular in northern India.
