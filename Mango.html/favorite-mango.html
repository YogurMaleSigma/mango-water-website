Favorite Mango Varieties

1. Alphonso

Known as the "King of Mangoes," Alphonso mangoes are celebrated for their sweetness and rich flavor. They have a smooth texture and a vibrant yellow-orange color. Primarily grown in India, they are perfect for smoothies, desserts, or simply enjoyed fresh.

2. Haden

Haden mangoes are juicy and sweet, with a slight tang. They feature a bright red blush over green and yellow skin. Haden mangoes are commonly used in salsas, salads, and as a topping for yogurt.

3. Kent

Kent mangoes are sweet, juicy, and fiberless, making them great for eating fresh. They are usually green and red when ripe and are often used in smoothies and fruit salads.
